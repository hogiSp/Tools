Privacy Policy

App: Spot-It Card Generator
Developer: Holger Specht, Bad Abbach, Germany
Last updated: May 1, 2026

✅ This app collects no personal data whatsoever.

1. Data Collection

The Spot-It Card Generator does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. No account is required. No registration is needed.

2. Data Storage

All data (uploaded images, card layouts, settings) is stored exclusively on your own device — either in your browser's local memory or in files you explicitly save yourself. Nothing is sent to any server.

3. Internet Access

The app loads its files from GitHub Pages (hogisp.github.io) on first launch and then works entirely offline. No analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party services are included.

4. Permissions

The Android app (TWA) requests no special permissions beyond basic internet access for the initial load.

5. Third-Party Services

No third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics tools, or crash-reporting services are used.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Given that no data is collected, significant changes are unlikely.
